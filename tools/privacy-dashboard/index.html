Privacy Dashboard PRO

Your one-glance status across every privacy tool — risk score, vault health, monitored emails, breach hits. Free tier shows local snapshots. Premium pulls live signals from monitoring + your synced devices.

What Powers This Dashboard

Each tool you use writes a small snapshot to your browser's localStorage — risk score, vault size, watched emails. The dashboard reads those snapshots. With Premium + sign-in, the same snapshots sync end-to-end encrypted across your devices, and the monitoring backend feeds in live breach alerts.
