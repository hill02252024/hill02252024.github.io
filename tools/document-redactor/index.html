    What Gets Detected

    • Email — RFC-ish: local@domain.tld
    • Phone — 7+ digits with separators (international and local formats)
    • SSN — US Social Security 123-45-6789
    • Credit card — 13–19 digit groups, with Luhn validation
    • IBAN — 2-letter country + 2 check digits + up to 30 alphanumeric
    • IP address — IPv4 + simplified IPv6
    • Names — capitalised consecutive words (heuristic, may over-match)
    • Custom — anything you list, case-insensitive
