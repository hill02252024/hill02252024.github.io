A zero-knowledge password vault that runs entirely in your browser. Your master passphrase derives the encryption key; we never see it. Free tier stores up to 10 entries locally. Premium unlocks unlimited entries and end-to-end encrypted cross-device sync.

How the Vault Works

Your master passphrase is fed through PBKDF2 (250,000 SHA-256 iterations) to derive a 256-bit AES-GCM key. Every entry is encrypted with a fresh random IV. The ciphertext lives in your browser's localStorage. Sync (Premium) uploads only the encrypted blob — our server sees ciphertext, not contents.

key = PBKDF2(passphrase, salt, 250k iter, SHA-256, 256 bit)
ciphertext = AES-GCM(key, iv, plaintext)

stored: { iv, ciphertext } ← never the passphrase
