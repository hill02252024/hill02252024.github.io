What is Today's Tasks?

Today's Tasks is a free, no-signup daily to-do list web app designed for busy professionals who want to organize their day in seconds. It runs entirely in your browser, stores data locally, and never sends your personal tasks to any server.

  • Set priorities: High / Due Today / General
  • Mark tasks completed and restore them later
  • Automatic midnight reset each day
  • Responsive design for desktop & mobile

Use Today's Tasks to streamline your workflow and boost your productivity. No ads in the app itself—just a clean interface to help you get things done.
