What is Today's Tasks?

Today's Tasks is a free, no-signup daily to-do list app designed for busy professionals who want to organize their day in seconds. It runs entirely in your browser, stores data locally, and never sends your personal tasks to any server.

  • Set priorities: High / Due Today / General
  • Mark tasks completed and restore them later
  • Automatic midnight reset each day
  • Responsive design for desktop and mobile

          Use Cases

          Busy professionals: park deep-work items in High, time-bound errands in Due Today, and keep a light buffer in General. Pair with our time-management guide.

          Students: log homework and exam prep by urgency. Review at night to reset for tomorrow—see tips in digital to-do lists.

          Freelancers: track client deliverables and invoices; move shipped tasks to Completed for a clean slate. Learn a weekly cadence in Weekly Review.

          Parents & households: capture chores and shopping, then reset daily to avoid clutter. Explore our time-saving templates.

          Small teams: use lanes as a lightweight stand-up—yesterday’s done, today’s focus, and upcoming items. See practical productivity tips.

          FAQ

          Do I need an account?

          No. Your list saves locally in your browser—zero sign-up.

          Is my data private?

          Yes. We never send your tasks to any server.

          Does it work offline?

          Yes. Tasks add/complete/restore offline and persist locally.

          How should I use the lanes?

          High = critical, Due Today = time-bound, General = everything else.

          What happens at midnight?

          The list resets to keep focus; finished items stay in Completed.

          Can I restore a finished task?

          Open Completed, then move the item back to a lane.

          Is it mobile friendly?

          Yes. The UI is responsive across phones, tablets, and desktops.
