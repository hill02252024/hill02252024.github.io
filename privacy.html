Who We Are

Today’s Tasks is a lightweight to-do web app focused on privacy and clarity. We do not require accounts and we keep your personal tasks on your device by default.

Related: About · Why digital to-do lists? · How to use the app

Information We Collect

  • Operational data: Basic, anonymous technical info (e.g., page views, approximate device type) used to understand performance and improve features.
  • Ad data: Third-party ad partners may process limited data to deliver and measure ads. See Third-Party Services.

Local-Only Tasks

Your to-do items are stored in your own browser’s LocalStorage. By default, we do not transmit task content to our servers.

To remove local data anytime, you can clear site storage for todays-tasks.com in your browser settings or use the app’s reset options (see How to Use).

Third-Party Services (Ads/Analytics)

We use Google services to serve ads and understand anonymous usage patterns. Please review their policies:

  • Google Ads & Technologies
  • Google Cookies
  • Manage ad personalization

You may also enable your browser’s tracking protection and disable third-party cookies to reduce personalization.

Cookies & Controls

We rely on minimal, third-party cookies for ad delivery and aggregate analytics. You can:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Use Google Ad Settings to opt out of personalized ads
  • Use private browsing or tracking-protection modes

Data Security

We apply industry-standard safeguards to protect site traffic and minimize data collection. Because task content is stored locally, you control it on your device.

Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Deletion: Clear site data in your browser to remove tasks and local settings.
  • Opt-out: Manage ad personalization via Google Ad Settings.
  • Contact: Have a privacy question? Contact us.

Children

Today’s Tasks is intended for general audiences and not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users

By using this site, you understand data may be processed by third-party providers in regions where they operate. We seek privacy-respecting defaults wherever possible.

Policy Updates

When this policy changes, we will update the date at the top. Material changes will be summarized here.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us via the Contact page.
