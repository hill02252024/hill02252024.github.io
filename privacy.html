Privacy Policy

Overview

Today’s Tasks is designed to be local-first. Your tasks are stored in your browser’s localStorage and are not sent to our servers. This section explains what is stored locally and what third-party services may be involved when ads are displayed.

Data We Store Locally

  • Tasks: task text and status (important / due today / general / completed).
  • Basic preferences: minor UI settings that help the app remember your choices.

You can clear your tasks at any time by using the app’s daily reset or by clearing your browser data.

Cookies & Advertising

Our website may display advertising provided by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites. You can manage ad personalization at adssettings.google.com and learn more at Google Ads Policies.

Children’s Privacy

Today’s Tasks is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • Clear tasks anytime via the app or browser storage settings.
  • Manage Google ad personalization using the links above.
