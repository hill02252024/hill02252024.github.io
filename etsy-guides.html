FAQ

Do I need Microsoft 365 or a Google account?

You can use either Excel (Microsoft 365 or Excel 2021+) or Google Sheets (requires a free Google account). Both versions are included on Etsy.

Is it beginner-friendly?

Yes. Dropdowns, color indicators, and pre-built charts make it easy. You mainly type tasks and check off completions—everything else updates automatically.

Can I print our weekly plan?

Yes. Use the Weekly Planner or Calendar View tabs for clean, printable layouts.
