About Today's Tasks

Our Mission

Today’s Tasks exists to help busy professionals focus on what truly matters. We remove friction from daily planning so you can capture the next action, set clear priorities, and get back to meaningful work. No accounts, no complicated setup—just a lightweight list that works anywhere.

Who We Are

We’re a small team of developers and productivity enthusiasts. We value speed, clarity, and privacy. Our philosophy is to build useful tools that feel invisible: fast to load, easy to use, and respectful of your time and data.

What We Offer

  • Daily To-Do List: Plan and prioritize your day in seconds with three simple lanes—High Priority, Due Today, and General.
  • Local-Only Storage: Your tasks stay in your browser’s LocalStorage by default. Nothing is sent to our servers.
  • Helpful Utilities: Extras like a FIRE Calculator and Image Resizer to support your workflow without installing apps.
  • Responsive Design: Works smoothly on desktop and mobile so you can review your list anywhere.

Why Choose Us

We focus on essentials: fast performance, clear UI, and privacy-first choices. We ship small, frequent improvements and listen to feedback. If a feature doesn’t help you finish work sooner, we don’t add it.

What’s Next

We’re exploring gentle enhancements—keyboard shortcuts, optional export, and accessibility refinements—while keeping the app simple. If you have ideas, we’d love to hear from you on the Contact page.
