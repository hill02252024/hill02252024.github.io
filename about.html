About Today's Tasks

Our Mission

We created Today’s Tasks to help busy professionals focus on what truly matters. No signups, no clutter—just a clean, effective daily to-do list that stores your tasks locally in your browser.

Who We Are

We’re a small team of developers and productivity enthusiasts passionate about building fast, reliable, and easy-to-use web tools. Our goal is to make productivity software that gets out of the way and lets you focus on your work.

What We Offer

  • Daily To-Do List: Organize and prioritize your day in seconds.
  • FIRE Calculator: Estimate your financial independence path.
  • Image Resizer: Quickly batch-resize photos without installing anything.

Why Choose Us

Our tools are lightweight, responsive, and privacy-first. We listen to user feedback, release updates regularly, and never overload our tools with unnecessary features. Productivity, without distraction.
