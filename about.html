About Today's Tasks

Our Mission

We built Today’s Tasks to help busy professionals focus on meaningful work. Many tools are heavy and require sign-ups, teams, and integrations. Today’s Tasks takes the opposite approach: a fast, private, no-signup to-do list that runs entirely in your browser.

What Makes It Different

  • Zero setup: open the site and start typing—no accounts, no sync headaches.
  • Local-first: tasks are saved in your browser’s localStorage; your data stays on your device.
  • Just enough structure: three lanes—High Priority, Due Today, and General—keep decisions simple.
  • Daily reset: at midnight your list clears so you start fresh, avoiding task bloat.
  • Accessible: readable typography and keyboard-friendly interactions.

How We Think About Productivity

Productivity is about attention and momentum, not managing dozens of labels. We optimize for speed: capture the next visible step, choose a lane, and get moving. The less time you spend managing the tool, the more time you spend doing the work.

Roadmap

  • Optional export/import of tasks for backups.
  • Light and dark themes.
  • Keyboard shortcuts for power users.

We keep the roadmap intentionally small to preserve the app’s simplicity.
