About Today's Tasks

Our Mission

We built Today’s Tasks to help busy professionals focus on what really matters. No signup, no clutter—just a simple daily to-do list that stores your tasks locally in your browser.

Who We Are

We’re a small team of developers and productivity enthusiasts dedicated to creating lightning-fast, easy-to-use web tools that stay out of your way and let you get things done.

What We Offer

  • Daily To-Do List: Plan and prioritize your day in seconds.
  • FIRE Calculator: Estimate your path to financial independence.
  • Image Resizer: Quickly batch-resize photos without installing anything.

Why Choose Us

Our tools are lightweight, responsive, and privacy-first. We listen to feedback, ship improvements regularly, and avoid unnecessary complexity so you can stay focused on meaningful work.
