Weekly Review Checklist

Why a Weekly Review Matters

A weekly review helps you clear your mind, evaluate what worked, and set priorities for the week ahead. Without reflection, tasks pile up and productivity suffers.

Step 1: Clear Completed Tasks

Archive tasks marked as complete. A clean slate keeps your list relevant and prevents clutter from distracting you.

Step 2: Re-evaluate Priorities

Look at what’s left in High, Due Today, and General. Move items around if their urgency or importance has changed.

Step 3: Capture Loose Ends

Think about emails, conversations, or projects from the past week. Write them down as new tasks so nothing gets forgotten.

Step 4: Plan Top Three Goals

Decide on three outcomes that would make the coming week a success. These become your guiding priorities when the week gets busy.

Step 5: Reflect and Improve

Ask yourself: What slowed me down? What boosted my progress? Small tweaks compound into big productivity gains over time.

Bring It All Together

Use Today’s Tasks as the anchor for your review. The app’s simple lanes help you reset each week with clarity, focus, and momentum.
