Frequently Asked Questions

Is Debt-Free Plan free?
Yes — fully free, no premium tier.
Does it require a credit check or bank link?
No. No credit pull, no Plaid, no bank linking. You enter your balances and APRs manually.
Avalanche or snowball — which is better?
Avalanche saves more interest. Snowball builds more momentum. Debt-Free Plan shows you both, with the exact dollar and month gap.
Does it work offline?
Yes. All the math is on your phone. No server, no upload of your balances or APRs.
Can I model lump-sum payments?
Yes. Allocate a tax refund or bonus to either strategy and see the months saved.
Does it handle variable APR cards?
You can preview a rate-change scenario — 'what if my card jumps from 18.99% to 24.99%' — and see the new timeline.
