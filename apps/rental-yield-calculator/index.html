Frequently Asked Questions

Is the rental yield calculator free?
Yes — fully free with no ads.
What's the difference between gross yield, net yield, cap rate, and cash-on-cash ROI?
Gross yield is annual rent / price. Net yield subtracts operating expenses. Cap rate uses net operating income / price (institutional standard). Cash-on-cash ROI is annual cash flow / cash actually invested — the only one that accounts for leverage.
Does it support Hong Kong property tax?
Yes. HK property tax presets are built in, alongside US, UK, and EU defaults.
Can I model a mortgage?
Yes — leverage modelling factors PITI into the monthly cash flow so you see cash-on-cash ROI honestly.
Does it work offline?
Yes. All calculations are local. Currency conversion uses cached mid-market rates after first sync.
Can I compare multiple properties?
Yes — three side-by-side with all four key metrics visible.
